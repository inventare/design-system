# Container

Containers are used to contain, pad, and (sometimes) center the content within a given device or viewport.

> Our container blocks are directly inspired (~copied~) from Bootstrap,
> MIT licensed, source code. These docs are condensed and you can consult
> the original [Bootstrap documentation](https://getbootstrap.com/docs/5.3/layout/containers/).

We comes with three different containers:

- `<Container breakpoint="all" />`, which sets a `max-width` at each responsive breakpoint.
- `<Container breakpoint="{breakpoint}" />`, which is `width: 100%` until the specified breakpoint.
- `<Container breakpoint="fluid" />`, which is `width: 100%` at all breakpoints.

The table below illustrates how each container's `max-width` compares to the original `<Container />` and `<Container breakpoint="fluid" />` across each breakpoint.

| Component                          | Extra Small (< 576px) | Small (>= 576px) | Medium (>= 768px)  | Large (>= 992px) | X-Large (>= 1200px) | XX-Large (>= 1400px) |
| ---------------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------ | ---------------- | ------------------- | -------------------- |
| `<Container/>`                     | 100%                  | 540px            | 720px              | 960px            | 1140px              | 1320px               |
| `<Container breakpoint="sm" />`    | 100%                  | 540px            | 720px              | 960px            | 1140px              | 1320px               |
| `<Container breakpoint="md" />`    | 100%                  | 100%             | 720px              | 960px            | 1140px              | 1320px               |
| `<Container breakpoint="lg" />`    | 100%                  | 100%             | 100%               | 960px            | 1140px              | 1320px               |
| `<Container breakpoint="xl" />`    | 100%                  | 100%             | 100%               | 100%             | 1140px              | 1320px               |
| `<Container breakpoint="xxl" />`   | 100%                  | 100%             | 100%               | 100%             | 100%                | 1320px               |
| `<Container breakpoint="fluid" />` | 100%                  | 100%             | 100%               | 100%             | 100%                | 100%                 |
